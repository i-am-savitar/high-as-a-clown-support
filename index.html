Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Tilt your device left and right to move, and tap anywhere on the screen to boost upward. Collect balloons, dodge obstacles, and rise through three zones: Carnival, Clouds, and Space!
What are balloons for?
Balloons are your lives. You start with one and can hold up to four. Getting hit by an obstacle pops one balloon. If you lose all your balloons, it's game over!
How do I win?
Reach a score of 8,000 or higher to trigger the ISS docking cutscene and claim victory. Can you make it all the way to space?
Does it work on iPad?
Yes! High As A Clown runs on both iPhone and iPad. The game uses your device's accelerometer, so hold your iPad comfortably and tilt to steer.
Are there in-app purchases?
Nope. The game is completely free with no in-app purchases. Occasional ads help support development.
How do leaderboards work?
Leaderboards are powered by Apple Game Center. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your device settings, and your high scores will be submitted automatically.

Tips & Tricks

🎈 Stay near the center of the screen for more reaction time.
🌈 Each zone has different gravity — Space feels floatier!
🎈 Grab every balloon you see — extra lives are crucial in Space.
⏱ After getting hit, use your invincibility frames to reposition safely.
